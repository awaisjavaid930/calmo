Delete Your Account

At Calmo, you have full control over your account and data.

Delete from App

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Calmo app
  2. Tap on the User Icon (menu tab)
  3. Scroll to the bottom
  4. Tap "Delete Account"

Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

awaisjavaid93@gmail.com

Data Deletion

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, photo)
  • Your favorites and saved content
  • Your usage-related data

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Retention Policy

We do not retain any personal user data after deletion. All data is permanently erased from our systems.
